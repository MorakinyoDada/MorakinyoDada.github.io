About Me

I am an early-career Quality Assurance professional with over 15 years of experience in customer service and operational leadership across aviation and service-driven environments. My career has been shaped by roles that demanded precision, accountability, and strict adherence to standards, particularly in safety-critical operations.

Before transitioning into Quality Assurance, I served as a Senior Flight Attendant, Cabin Crew Supervisor, and Inflight Manager, where I enforced procedures, supervised teams, managed incidents, and ensured compliance in high-pressure, time-sensitive situations.

My transition into QA was intentional and skills-driven. I am building hands-on experience in manual testing, test planning, test case design, defect reporting, API testing, and SQL data validation. I bring a calm, methodical, and people-focused approach to QA, shaped by years of leadership and service excellence.
